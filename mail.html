Sales hacks...
Sales email copywriting can be a nightmare unless you’ve worked out how to do it right.
Write the way you talk to your friends It can be tempting to use big words to sound smarter but… You’re writing to a human, and your goal is to get your idea across not to show off your vocabulary. Replace all the “in order to”s, “due to the fact that”s and “hence”s with “to”s, “because”s and “that’s why”s.
Ditch the jargon; mirror the language your audience uses If the recipient of your email doesn’t use “email cadences”, don’t be a showoff — stick to “email sequences” and “email follow-ups”.
